Psalms 131

1 Yahweh, my heart isn`t haughty, nor my eyes lofty; Neither do I concern myself with great matters, Or things too wonderful for me. 2 Surely I have stilled and quieted my soul, Like a weaned child with his mother, Like a weaned child is my soul within me. 3 Israel, hope in Yahweh, From this time forth and forevermore. Psalm 132 A Song of Ascents.
