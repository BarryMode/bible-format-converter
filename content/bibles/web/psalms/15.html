Psalms 15

1 Yahweh, who shall dwell in your sanctuary? Who shall live on your holy hill? 2 He who walks blamelessly, does what is right, And speaks truth in his heart; 3 He who doesn`t slander with his tongue, Nor does evil to his friend, Nor casts slurs against his fellow man; 4 In whose eyes a vile man is despised, But who honors those who fear Yahweh; He who keeps an oath even when it hurts, and doesn`t change; 5 He who doesn`t lend out his money for usury, Nor take a bribe against the innocent. He who does these things shall never be shaken. Psalm 16 A Poem by David.
