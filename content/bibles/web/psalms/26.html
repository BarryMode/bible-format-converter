Psalms 26

1 Judge me, Yahweh, for I have walked in my integrity. I have trusted also in Yahweh without wavering. 2 Examine me, Yahweh, and prove me. Try my heart and my mind. 3 For your lovingkindness is before my eyes. I have walked in your truth. 4 I have not sat with deceitful men, Neither will I go in with hypocrites. 5 I hate the assembly of evil-doers, And will not sit with the wicked. 6 I will wash my hands in innocence, So I will go about your altar, Yahweh; 7 That I may make the voice of thanksgiving to be heard, And tell of all your wondrous works. 8 Yahweh, I love the habitation of your house, The place where your glory dwells. 9 Don`t gather my soul with sinners, Nor my life with bloodthirsty men; 10 In whose hands is wickedness, Their right hand is full of bribes. 11 But as for me, I will walk in my integrity. Redeem me, and be merciful to me. 12 My foot stands in an even place. In the congregations will I bless Yahweh. Psalm 27 By David.
